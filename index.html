What is this all about?

Well, this site is about making and optimizing your games made in UPBGE or Range.
UPBGE is a game engine forked by its developers from Blender 2.79's game engine which was discontinued after Blender 2.80+. The engine has two version as 2.0+ which is the Blender's 2.79's branch and 4.0 which is 2.80+'s branch. After a few years, UPBGE 2.x development was paused/discontinued as 2.5b and a new project rised up, the Range Engine which is being actively developed and maintained by its developers!

After a few months, UPBGE 2.6 by Kitsuy is being developed seriously with much more bug fixes ending up more like next stable version of UPBGE 2.5b.
